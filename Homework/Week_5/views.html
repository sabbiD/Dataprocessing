Patenting around the world.

These visualisations below show the amount of patent applications around the world. The map on the left visualises the income group the country is a part of, while the scatter plot shows how much of their income this country has spent on research (GERD) in relation to the amount of patent applications in 2015. By hovering over the map you can see the information for every country. By clicking on the rectangles of the legend the specific dot for that country will light up or you can click on the country in the map and the dot will also light up (if data is available). Also by clicking the continents button you can choose to view the map and the scatter plot for another continent. For more information and the sources you can go to the background page.
